The P2P Scam Crisis & Our Defense

Informal peer-to-peer markets are rife with fake receipts, chargebacks, and double-spending. Paymax redefines P2P around absolute trust.

Informal P2P Markets (High Risk)

  • Fake Payment Evidence: Scammers send photoshopped transaction screenshots or fake SMS alerts to trick sellers.
  • No Identity Association: Strangers disappear after scamming, creating new fake accounts.
  • Chargeback Fraud: Buyers cancel banking transactions after the cryptocurrency is released.

Paymax Verified Marketplace (Zero Scams)

  • Smart SMS Parsing: Buyers paste bank notification text directly. Paymax verifies receipt structures to prevent fake evidence.
  • Circle of Trust: Network growth is invite-only and reputation-linked. Inviting scammers degrades your own trust score.
  • Liveness & KYC: Every user undergoes liveness checks and government ID validation before placing trades.

Ecosystem Architecture

Four integrated layers working in synchronization to guarantee absolute trading security.

1. Escrow Protection

4. Fingerprint & AI Auditing

Escalating limits, device hash monitoring, and rapid transfer flags isolate fraud vectors instantly.
